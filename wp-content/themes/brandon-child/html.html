Maple Hosiery Mills Ltd. represents a strong Canadian identity and we remain committed to this vision.

Our Canadian headquarters continues to support our retail partners by providing knowledgeable expertise and service in:

  • sales
  • product design & development
  • point of sale
  • sampling
  • automatic replenishment
  • inventory warehouse
  • and shipping with an operational manufacturing capacity.

Maple Hosiery played a major role in the introduction of private brands and emerged as a leading domestic supplier, and the best kept secret...

While not highly recognized by our consumers, we work passionately to distribute quality men's and women's dress, casual, athletic and work socks.

We are driven by our vision and mission to deliver value and service in a quality sock for every occasion, appealing to all lifestyles. More specifically, we encourage creativity and innovation throughout our entire supply chain and bring inspiration and special features to corporate brands, private label programs and licensed product.

Over the past four decades, we have strengthened our core manufacturing capabilities

by establishing a competitive edge through vertical integration strategies and setting up key partnerships in North America, Asia and Europe.

Our consistent growth is rooted in

  • strong core values
  • innovation
  • and product integrity
